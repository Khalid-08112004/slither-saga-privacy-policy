1. Introduction

Welcome to Slither Saga (“we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game. By downloading or playing this game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly from users. However, certain third-party services integrated into the game may automatically collect non-personal information.

(a) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • Gameplay analytics (session length, screens viewed)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Crash logs and performance data

(b) Ad Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and related technologies to:

  • Serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad
  • Measure ad performance

Learn more here: Google AdMob & AdSense Privacy Policy

3. How We Use the Information

  • Improve gameplay experience and stability
  • Display relevant ads
  • Analyze performance and user engagement

We never sell or share your personal information.

4. Children’s Privacy

Slither Saga is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected data. However, no internet-based service is completely secure.

6. Your Choices
